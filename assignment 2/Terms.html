Goodreads is a social platform where users can share and discover book recommendations, engage
with a diverse community of readers, and track their reading progress. Members can rate, review,
and discuss books, participate in groups, and create reading lists based on personal preferences or
genres. The platform's recommendation system helps users find new titles based on their reading
history and the suggestions of others. Goodreads also features author profiles, book clubs, and
various reading challenges, making it a comprehensive space for book lovers to connect, explore,
and expand their literary horizons.
